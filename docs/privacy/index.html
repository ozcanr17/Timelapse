Flapse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Summary

Flapse does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your photos and videos stay on your device.

Photos and videos

Photos you capture and timelapse videos you create are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud backup, they are stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's iCloud terms; we have no access to them.

Photo library access

If you import photos from your library, Flapse reads only the photos you explicitly select, plus their capture date and location metadata, to place them on your project timeline. Nothing is uploaded to us.

Camera and location

Camera access is used only to take your progress photos. Optional location access is used only to tag your own captures; tags stay on your device.

Analytics and tracking

Flapse contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers. We collect no usage data.

Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Sign in with Apple

If you sign in, we receive only the anonymous identifier Apple provides, used solely to enable collaboration features you initiate.
