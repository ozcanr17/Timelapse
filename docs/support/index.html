FAQ

Where are my photos stored? On your device (and your personal iCloud if you enable backup).

How do I restore a purchase? Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app.

How do I recover a deleted project? Settings → Recently Deleted, within 30 days (timelapses: 7 days).
